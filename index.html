Emily, 7 years

Diagnosis: Spinal Muscular Atrophy Type 1

Emily was diagnosed with SMA Type 1 at 6 months old. This genetic disorder affects motor neurons, causing muscle weakness and respiratory problems. She needs Zolgensma, a one-time gene therapy treatment that costs $2.8 million CAD.

David, 29 years

Diagnosis: Glioblastoma Multiforme

David was diagnosed with an aggressive brain tumor. Standard treatments in the UK have been unsuccessful. He needs experimental immunotherapy at a specialized clinic in Germany costing £250,000. Time is critical as the tumor continues to grow.

Raised: £120,000 Goal: £250,000

Aisha, 35 years

Diagnosis: Multiple Sclerosis (Progressive)

Aisha's MS has progressed rapidly, leaving her unable to walk. She needs HSCT (stem cell transplant) treatment in Mexico costing $75,000 USD, which could halt disease progression. As a single mother of two, she can't afford this life-changing treatment.

Raised: $32,000 USD Goal: $75,000 USD
